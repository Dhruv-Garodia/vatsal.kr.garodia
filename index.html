About Me

I’m currently with Dream Capital, an early-stage growth equity fund investing in high-potential software companies across the U.S. My work focuses on conducting financial and operating analyses, evaluating SaaS metrics, and performing qualitative diligence to support investment decisions.

Previously, I interned at Arc Ventures, a venture capital firm, where I supported due diligence on early-stage investments, built financial models to guide portfolio allocation and pro-rata decisions, and prepared fund deployment agendas to inform capital allocation. I also prepared equity research reports for a portfolio of 70+ public companies to inform reallocation decisions and portfolio strategy.

I’ve completed all three levels of the CFA Program and hold an MS in Finance from Fordham University, where I was honored with the Academic Area Award for Excellence in Business, Finance, and Economics and named to the Dean’s List. These experiences have helped me develop a well-rounded skill set spanning financial modeling, valuation, scenario and sensitivity analysis, investment analysis, due diligence, market research, and strategic thinking. I’m excited to build on this foundation and continue delivering value in future roles while expanding my impact across the fields of investing, strategy, and finance.
